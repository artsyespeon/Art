Markers


  • If your kids are begining art you should provide them with a sheet of paper and some washable colored markers

    Markers are easier to use than colored pencils but more messy

    Their are also expert markers in stores, do not give them to starters

    Brush tip markers are fun to use and awesome to write with

    If I could choose one brand of marker it would be ohuhu, their markers are brush tiped and double sided!
